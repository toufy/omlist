inception

( action | sci-fi | adventure )

a brilliant take on the "dream reading" concept.
it's hard to talk about this movie without spoiling it, but the story revolves around corporate espionage through inception.
instead of using the dream sharing technology to pull information, cobb tries to implant an idea in fischer's mind.
the movie explores multi-level dreams (dreams within dreams), and jumping back and forth through dreams and reality.
the characters have unique totems that let them diffirentiate between dreams and real life.
