mr. robot

( crime | drama )

my favorite series. this is a masterpiece.
the series highlights the thin line between good and bad, and focuses a lot on the concept of control.
elliot and mr. robot's monologues are the best in tv history, and almost all of the other characters, even some secondary ones, have legendary lines.
even the antagonists are very lovable, and you can almost sympathize with many of them.
it also has the most accurate portrayal of hacking out of any movie or tv show, if we ignore how fast and easy they make the hacks seem.
