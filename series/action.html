the boys

( sci-fi | fantasy | action | adventure )

a superhero series with an original take.
it is a little dark and offensive while still being very funny, which is something not many series manage to pull off.
usually with "edgy" action/comedy series/movies we either end up with something very bland with no action, or very offensive but unfunny.
the boys managed to balance the two, while giving it a superhero twist.
